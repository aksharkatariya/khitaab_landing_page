About & How it works

Just speak. We take it from there.

Voice-enabled

Khitaab is an AI financial assistant built specifically for farmers. Forget apps, spreadsheets, or accounting knowledge — you simply speak into your phone in your own language, and Khitaab handles the rest.

Supporting 9 Indian languages, farmers can record a voice message saying what they spent or earned, or just type it naturally. Khitaab understands both. The name itself means address or speech — a direct conversation between you and your finances.

At any moment, ask for a report, question a decision, or get seasonal budgeting advice — all through simple conversation in the language you think in.
