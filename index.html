What is Khitaab?

Khitaab is an AI-powered chatbot designed with farmers in mind. It removes the complexity from farm financial management: no spreadsheets, no apps to learn, no accounting knowledge required.

You simply send a text message describing what you spent or earned, and Khitaab keeps track of it all. At any point, you can ask for a full financial report or get guidance on financial decisions specific to your farm.

The name Khitaab means address or speech, a direct conversation between you and your finances.

How it works

1

Open the chat

Find Khitaab on Telegram. No sign-up forms or downloads beyond Telegram itself.

2

Log your expenses in plain language

Type something like "spent 800 on fertiliser today" or "sold wheat for 4500." Khitaab understands natural language, no special format needed.

3

Request a report anytime

Ask Khitaab for a summary of your expenses, income, or profit for any time period. It will generate a clear breakdown for you.

4

Ask financial questions

Not sure whether to take out a loan for equipment? Wondering how to budget for the next season? Ask Khitaab and get practical, context-aware advice.

Features

Financial reports
